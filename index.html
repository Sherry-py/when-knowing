Stream 04 · Continuous Governance Architecture · A symbiotic architecture for human–LLM–methodology systems · In progress
Reliable governance is a structural property of the action channel, not an event at decision moments. Current AI safety architectures govern at the platform layer (model providers, API guardrails); the deploying organization absorbs the residual — a mismatch between governance locus and accountability locus.
可靠的治理是行动通道本身的结构属性，而非决策时刻的事件。当前 AI 安全架构治理停留在平台层（模型提供方、API 护栏）；部署方组织承担一切残余风险 —— 治理归属与问责归属在结构上错位。
Continuous Governance Architecture composes closed-loop authorization harnesses (continuous-time self-correction, à la Paper 1) with commit points (discrete-time boundary attestation; Weill & Ross, 2004). Requisite-variety reasoning (Ashby, 1956) makes both layers necessary: platform-layer guardrails cannot match the environmental variety of agentic action.
连续治理架构由两层组合而成：闭环授权 harness（连续时间的自校正，沿用 Paper 1）与 commit point（离散时间的边界证 attestation；Weill & Ross, 2004）。按 requisite-variety 原理（Ashby, 1956），平台层护栏的控制变量无法匹配 agentic 行动的环境变量。

立场

Cybernetics, re-instantiated.

控制论的再具身

The problem of the agent era is not new in form — it is the problem Wiener (1948) identified at the birth of cybernetics: perception decoupled from action. What changes is the sensor. In classical control systems the sensor is external to the controlled object. In agentic systems the sensor is the agent's own diagnostic self-report. This is the formally novel piece, and the reason a two-layer architecture — closed-loop harness with commit-point attestation — is required.

Agent 时代的问题在形式上并不新 —— 它正是 Wiener (1948) 在控制论诞生时指认的：感知与行动的解耦。变化在于传感器。经典控制系统中，传感器外在于被控对象；agentic 系统中，传感器就是 agent 自己的诊断自报。这是形式上真正新的部分，也是为什么需要双层架构 —— 闭环 harness 加 commit-point 证明。

UNSW ISTM is the natural home for this work: it sits where principal-agent theory and design science meet behavioural experiments on how humans actually operate these gates.

UNSW ISTM 是这个研究的天然归处 —— 委托代理理论、设计科学，与"人类如何实际操作这些 gate"的行为实验在这里交汇。

关于

Xirui (Sherry) Lian designs cognitive scaffolding for agentic AI systems — structural methodology that complements LLM capability rather than constraining it. Currently developing GateFix, a structural mechanism that translates an LLM's diagnostic awareness into reliable execution behavior, grounded in nine years of climate-governance building. Previously co-launched ICECI at COP28 — a UNFCCC-registered initiative endorsed by China's Special Envoy for Climate Change Xie Zhenhua and recognised by UN ESCAP as a regional model for ICT-enabled climate governance — and built Lvdoya, a carbon-behavior platform deployed across 15 Chinese cities with 2.3M+ behavioral interactions. Practitioner delegate at six consecutive UN climate conferences (COP24–COP29). Affiliated with UNSW ISTM; working paper under review at Information & Management.

A decade of multi-stakeholder governance work produced a single methodological commitment: reliable system behavior is a structural property of the action channel, not an event at decision moments. GateFix instantiates this as cognitive scaffolding — structural support that complements an LLM's diagnostic capability. Continuous Governance Architecture generalizes it into a three-party symbiotic architecture (LLM + methodology + human).

连希蕊（Sherry Lian）设计面向 agentic AI 系统的认知支架 —— 一种与 LLM 能力相辅相成而非加以限制的结构化方法论。当前正在开发 GateFix —— 一个把 LLM 诊断能力转化为可靠执行行为的结构性机制，建立在九年气候治理实战之上。此前在 COP28 联合发起 ICECI（UNFCCC 注册倡议，获中国气候变化特使解振华背书，被联合国亚太经社会列为 ICT 赋能气候治理区域典范），并搭建 Lvdoya —— 部署于中国 15 座城市、累计 230 万+ 行为交互的碳行为平台。连续六届联合国气候大会（COP24–COP29）实操代表。隶属 UNSW ISTM；工作论文在 Information & Management 审稿中。

十年的多方治理实践得出一个方法论承诺：可靠的系统行为是行动通道本身的结构属性，而非决策时刻的事件。GateFix 把这一承诺落地为认知支架 —— 一种与 LLM 诊断能力相辅相成的结构性支撑。连续治理架构（Continuous Governance Architecture）则把它推广为 LLM + 方法论 + 人三方共生的架构。
