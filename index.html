The agent knows the context is broken. It acts anyway.

代理知道上下文有问题。它仍然继续执行。

LLM agents accurately diagnose defective context — yet authorize execution anyway. I formalize this diagnostic–authorization gap as structural routing failure: a previously uncharacterized IS governance failure mode whose remedy is not a better model but an execution harness — GateFix, a pre-execution governance layer grounded in principal–agent theory. A within-subject experiment across 500 financial reasoning tasks and 4 frontier LLMs reveals the gap as a robust cross-model behavioral pattern, and GateFix delivers a ~45 percentage-point reduction in false authorization on defective contexts (from ~50% to under 5% on capable models).

LLM 代理能准确识别上下文缺陷 —— 却仍然继续授权执行。我把这种诊断–授权裂隙形式化为结构性路由失败：一种此前尚未被系统刻画的 IS 治理失败模式 —— 它的对策不是更好的模型，而是一个执行 harness：GateFix，一种基于委托–代理理论的执行前治理层。跨 500 项金融推理任务与 4 个前沿 LLM 的被试内实验揭示出这一裂隙是一种稳健的跨模型行为模式，GateFix 在缺陷上下文上把错误授权率下降约 45 个百分点（从约 50% 降至 5% 以下，在能力达标的模型上）。

The GateFix mechanism

GateFix 的工作机制

Four dimensions. Two are critical — failure blocks execution outright. Two are non-critical — failure requests clarification. Together they produce one of three verdicts.

四个维度。两个关键维度失败则直接阻止执行，两个非关键维度失败则请求澄清。组合产生三种授权裁决之一。

These four dimensions are derived from Wang & Strong's (1996) contextual data quality subset, specialized for the moment of action authorization — that is, made action-relative rather than retrospective. Critical dimensions capture properties whose failure makes execution structurally invalid; non-critical dimensions capture properties whose failure makes execution unreliable but recoverable.

这四个维度由 Wang & Strong (1996) 提出的语境化数据质量子集派生而来，针对行动授权这一时刻进行专门化 —— 即从事后回顾转为面向行动。关键维度刻画的是其失败将使执行在结构上无效的属性；非关键维度刻画的则是失败会让执行不可靠但可恢复的属性。

What the research found

研究发现了什么

Within-subject experiment across 4 commercial LLMs and 3 financial benchmarks (TAT-QA, ConvFinQA, FinQA), 500 reasoning tasks. The diagnostic–authorization gap appears as a stable behavioral pattern across all models — robust to architecture, training corpus, and benchmark. GateFix reduces false authorization on defective contexts from ~50% to under 5% — a structural routing fix, not a model improvement. Diagnostic capability is necessary but not sufficient: only structure converts it into safe behaviour, with task abstraction (per Construal Level Theory) moderating intervention strength.

在 4 个商业 LLM 与 3 个金融基准（TAT-QA、ConvFinQA、FinQA）上的被试内实验，500 项推理任务。诊断–授权裂隙作为跨所有模型的稳定行为模式出现 —— 对架构、训练语料、基准均稳健。GateFix 把错误授权率从约 50% 降至 5% 以下 —— 这是结构性路由修复，不是模型能力提升。诊断能力是必要而非充分条件：只有结构性机制才能把它转化为安全行为；任务抽象水平（基于构念水平理论 / Construal Level Theory）调节干预强度。

Research roadmap · next 18 months

研究路线图 · 未来 18 个月

This is the first paper of a multi-paper programme on execution-layer governance for AI-enabled organizational systems.

这是"AI 赋能组织系统执行层治理"系列论文的第一篇。

Paper 1 establishes the micro-mechanism — structural routing failure as the smallest unit of misgovernance. Stream 04 is where the programme is currently developing: a meso-architecture composing closed-loop authorization harnesses (continuous time) with commit points (discrete time). Streams 01–03 extend the micro-mechanism along behavioural, cross-domain, and theoretical axes. The roadmap is a working map of where the thinking is heading, not a fixed plan.

Paper 1 确立微观机制 —— 结构性路由失败作为最小治理失败单元。Stream 04 是当前正在展开的方向：一个中观架构，将闭环授权 harness（连续时间）与 commit point（离散时间）组合在一起。Streams 01–03 则沿行为、跨域、理论三条轴线延伸这一微观机制。此路线图是当前思考的工作版图，并非已定下的计划。

Stream 04 · Continuous Governance Architecture · In progress
Reliable governance is a structural property of the action channel, not an event at decision moments. Current AI safety architectures govern at the platform layer (model providers, API guardrails); the deploying organization absorbs the residual — a mismatch between governance locus and accountability locus.
可靠的治理是行动通道本身的结构属性，而非决策时刻的事件。当前 AI 安全架构治理停留在平台层（模型提供方、API 护栏）；部署方组织承担一切残余风险 —— 治理归属与问责归属在结构上错位。
Continuous Governance Architecture composes closed-loop authorization harnesses (continuous-time self-correction, à la Paper 1) with commit points (discrete-time boundary attestation; Weill & Ross, 2004). Requisite-variety reasoning (Ashby, 1956) makes both layers necessary: platform-layer guardrails cannot match the environmental variety of agentic action.
连续治理架构由两层组合而成：闭环授权 harness（连续时间的自校正，沿用 Paper 1）与 commit point（离散时间的边界证 attestation；Weill & Ross, 2004）。按 requisite-variety 原理（Ashby, 1956），平台层护栏的控制变量无法匹配 agentic 行动的环境变量。

研究者视角

Why this moment. Why IS.

为什么是这个时刻，为什么是 IS

The governance question this research engages — when does AI capability translate into safe execution? — is increasingly visible across both scholarly and industry conversations.

本研究所追问的治理问题 —— AI 的能力何时才能转化为安全的执行？ —— 正在学术界与产业界的讨论中同时浮现。

"If you, like many of you, dedicate yourselves night and day to mastering these tools and combine that with your deep creativity, you will create things that are truly extraordinary."

"如果像你们中的许多人那样日以继夜地钻研这些工具，并将其与深层的创造力结合，就能创造出更不可思议的作品。"

— Demis Hassabis, co-founder & CEO, Google DeepMind

Hassabis frames the coming decade as human–AI symbiosis — AI amplifying human judgment, with humans providing the creativity, taste, and authorization machines cannot supply from within. The architecture that makes such symbiosis possible is not a model problem. It is a governance problem.

Hassabis 把未来十年定性为人机共生时代 —— AI 放大人的判断，由人提供创造力、品位与授权，这是机器无法从内部供给的。而支撑这种共生的架构，不是模型问题，而是治理问题。

2024–now · Agents

Agent era ← we are here

Agent 时代 ← 我们在这里

AI acts. The knowing–doing gap closes — but authorization is now the critical uncoupled variable. Governance failures become execution failures in production systems at scale.

AI 开始行动。知与行之间的裂隙弥合 — 但授权成为关键的未耦合变量。治理失败直接变为生产系统中的执行失败，且以规模发生。

Why IS is the right discipline for this problem
为什么 IS 是解决这个问题的正确学科

CS asks what AI can do; management asks how humans adapt; IS asks how organizational rules and technical capabilities get structurally coupled — and when that coupling fails. The diagnostic–authorization gap is precisely an IS failure mode: the right capability, the right rules, and the right intent, but not routed to meet. No other discipline holds both principal–agent governance theory and design science method to address that coupling directly.

计算机科学问 AI 能做什么；管理学问人类如何适应；IS 问的是组织规则与技术能力如何结构性耦合 — 以及耦合何时失败。诊断–授权裂隙恰恰是 IS 式的失败：能力、规则、意图都在，却没有被路由到一起。没有任何其他学科同时拥有委托–代理治理理论与设计科学方法来直接处理这个问题。

Over the next few years, I'm interested in whether execution-layer AI governance can stand as a coherent programme within IS — connecting agent-level mechanism design, organizational governance architecture, and behavioural evidence on how humans operate these gates in practice.

未来几年，我感兴趣的是：执行层 AI 治理（execution-layer AI governance）能否在 IS 内部站立为一条连贯的研究线 —— 把 agent 级机制设计、组织级治理架构、以及人类在这些"治理门"前实际行为的实证证据连成一体。
